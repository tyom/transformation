Transforming digital health and care

NHS is designing a digital health and care service that better connects patients to the information and services they need. The user is at the heart of everything we do.

NHS.UK projects form the building blocks of a new digital health and care service. A service that can deliver the next generation of digital healthcare, help reduce pressure on the NHS, improve efficiency and increase quality for all.

Our current focus:

1

NHS.UK

Transforming NHS Choices to deliver the next generation of digital services with relevant, practical, action-focused content and smart sign-posting to services.
2

Digital service standards

Publishing service standards and practical guidance to help teams deliver quality, safe and trusted digital services.
3

Register with a GP

An exemplar digital service designed to simplify the process for patients and help to reduce pressure on key NHS services.
4

Partnerships

Working in partnership with digital healthcare teams, across organisational boundaries, to drive quality and improve digital take-up.

The user at the heart of NHS.UK

Our work is research-led and focuses on the needs of patients, carers and front-line staff. We constantly iterate. Our work starts small, is tested and evolves based on users’ feedback. We make things open. We want to share, learn and adapt. We work in partnership with digital health and care teams and across organisational boundaries.
